About Me

I'm an academic leader and educational technology strategist with 27 years of experience driving innovation in higher education. Currently serving as Associate Director, Business Improvement Solutions and Principal Lecturer at the University of Westminster, I lead strategic initiatives across the institution, combining pedagogical expertise with technical innovation to deliver real-world impact.

My academic leadership experience includes serving as Assistant Head of School and Director for Learning and Teaching (2022-2025) in Computer Science and Engineering, where I led teaching innovation, AI integration in education, and student engagement initiatives. As a Senior Fellow of the Higher Education Academy and Non-Executive Director at Blueshift Education CIC, I champion evidence-based pedagogical approaches and educational transformation.

I'm a recognised expert speaker at Anthology/Blackboard conferences, presenting on AI integration, assessment innovation, and educational technology to international audiences across Europe and Africa. My presentations focus on practical implementations of emerging technologies in education, demonstrating how strategic technical solutions can enhance learning and teaching.

What sets me apart is the rare combination of academic leadership experience and hands-on technical expertise. I don't just lead strategy; I build working solutions that bring ideas to life. My technical background includes serving as jQuery Mobile Team Member & Documentation Lead (2011-2016) and elected board member (2013-2016) of the jQuery Foundation, one of the world's most widely-used JavaScript libraries. I develop browser extensions, web applications, AI-enhanced tools, and automation systems, bridging the gap between pedagogical vision and technical implementation to deliver solutions that work in real university environments.

My philosophy: Learning & Teaching + Coding + Strategy = Real-World Impact in Education.
