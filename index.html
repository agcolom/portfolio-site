About Me

I'm an Associate Director, Business Improvement Solutions and Principal Lecturer at the University of Westminster with a passion for creating practical educational technology solutions. My philosophy: Learning & Teaching + Coding + Strategy = Real-World Impact in Education.

With 27 years in higher education, I lead strategic business improvement initiatives across the university, driving operational excellence and innovative solutions. Previously, as Assistant Head of School and Director for Learning and Teaching (2022-2025), I led teaching innovation, AI integration, and student engagement initiatives in Computer Science.

I'm also a Senior Fellow of the Higher Education Academy and Non-Executive Director at Blueshift Education CIC. From 2013-2016, I served as an elected board member of the jQuery Foundation, one of the world's most widely-used JavaScript libraries, and continue to lead their content team.

I specialise in browser extensions, web development, AI in education, and automation solutions that integrate seamlessly with existing university systems whilst supporting evidence-based teaching practices.
